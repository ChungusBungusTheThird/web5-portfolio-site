Lucky the Rabbit game

Lucky the Rabbit is a game made by me, Felipe and Merari in our Web 3 course. You play as Lucky, collecting as many fruit as possible and delivering them in the basket. There are trees and rotten fruit on the field so as the player, you must avoid collecting these rotten fruit and taking damage by running into trees. There is a shining carrot that can spawn and once consumed, Lucky gets a speed boost!

The idea of the game started off by me sketching out a little bunny character in my notebook. I then tried expanding the idea and mechanics of the game and show them to my teammates. I was in charge of making the game assets. Concept art and assets are seen bellow...
