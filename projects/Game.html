Lucky The Rabbit Game

Lucky the Rabbit is a game made by me, Felipe, and Merari in our Web 3 course. You play as Lucky, collecting as many fruits as possible and delivering them in the basket. There are trees and rotten fruit on the field, so be careful! Avoid colliding with trees and rotten fruit that can damage you by running into trees. There is a shining carrot that can spawn and once consumed, Lucky gets a speed boost!

The idea of the game started off by me sketching out a little bunny character in my notebook. I then tried expanding the idea and making character game assets and showing them to my teammates. I was in charge of making the game assets.
