Skateboarding adventure

Skateboarding adventure is the name of my 4 animations. In my Web 3 class, we had to create 4 animations in 4 different ways of your choosing.

For the first animation, I made a spreadsheet of a skater moving his foot to gain more speed on his skateboard. For the second animation, I made a frame by frame animation of the skater doing a flip trick in the air and turned it into a gif. For my last 2 animations, I made it so there are slight movements. The skater falling off his skateboard and hovering up and down the air. And the thought bubble floating around the the skater. However, I decided to remake my animations with more color and with some improved animations.
