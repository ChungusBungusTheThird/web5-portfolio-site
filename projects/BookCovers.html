Scott Pilgrim book redesigns

This project had to of been one of my favorites of the program. I had been super into the Scott Pilgrim graphic novels and when I heard that we had to do a project where we need to design/redesign book covers, I just knew I had to make new designs for those graphic novels. I made 3 designs covers for the first 3 volumes.

I made designs for the front and back covers. The Scott Pilgrim graphic novels has many different cover iterations so It was a little challenging to make something different yet not to separate. But I think I was successful in making something unique and distinct that doesnâ€™t branch of the main designs from previous official covers.

Front and back cover concept sketches

Here are the many sketch ideas I had in mind when redesigning the covers. I wanted to create a cover illustration that shows off an important part in the story. So I sketched out 3 variations each of the first 3 books and chose the one that I think works best. The back covers are also important moments that happen in the story so I wanted to add those as well.

Final book Redesigns
